About Me

Megan Barrera is a graduating senior from the University of Kansas. In May 2017, she will have obtained her Bachelor's of Arts in Communication Studies with a Business Minor, as well as a Certification in Global Awareness and a Certification in Arts Engagement. After graduation, she hopes to work in the field of technology as a project manager.
During her time in college, she maintained a part time job working for a software development company that specialized in higher education health science software solutions. Throughout her two year internship, she established professional skills working with project implementations, databases, and client relations. As an intern, she planned, implemented, and successfully executed the company's first ever 3-day conference hosted in Kansas City. She also shifted the company's training efforts from help manuals to short tutorial videos which she edited, managed, and later implemented into the systems. Through proven communication skills and a knack for learning quickly, Megan has excelled at adapting to every situation she encounters.
In her free time, Megan has taken an interest in learning how to code. She has created various professional websites including an online portfolio showcasing her skills, work experience, and recent projects. To learn more, please visit www.MeganEBarrera.com. If you would like to get in touch, she can be contacted directly at megan.barrera1@gmail.com.
